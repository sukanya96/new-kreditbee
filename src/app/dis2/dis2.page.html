Loan Disbursal

I am unable to sign the loan agreement


Loan agreement can be signed post you select a bank account for disbursal.While avaling any of the CreditMonk cash loans you can sign the agreement using two ways:-

1.E-sign using Digio:- To sign using this method you need to have access to your aadhaar linked mobile number. An OTP will be sent on that number by entering which you can sign the agreement.

2.Sign using OTP:-An OTP will be sent on your CreditMonk registered mobile number and you can submit the same to get your agreement signed.

While availing any of the shop now products through CreditMonk you can sign the agreement by E-sign using the agreement by E-sign using Digio method.To sign using this method you need to have access to your aadhaar linked mobile number.An OTP will be sent on that number by entering which you can sign the agreement.
