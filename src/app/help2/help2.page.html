About Proascent

What is Proascent?

Proascent is an Instant Personal Loan Platform for individuals, where you can apply for a Loan of up to ₹ 2 Lakh. Proascent acts as a technology intermediary and facilitates this Personal Loans from regulated lenders to individuals. The documentation is very minimal, and the entire process starting from registration to disbursement does not take more than 15 minutes. The application process is completely online, and on approval, the cash is immediately transferred to the bank account of the user.

Who can apply for a loan in Proascent?

Proascent is an Instant Personal Loan Platform for individuals, where you can apply for a Loan of up to ₹ 2 Lakh. Any Indian individual above the age of 21 years, who is salaried or has a monthly source of income may apply for a Personal Loan through the Proascent Platform.

How does Proascent work?

Proascent is a technology platform which helps individuals avail Personal Loan through regulated lending partners. Applying for a Personal Loan through the proascent Platform is simple, quick and easy. Just follow the below steps: a. Install the proascent app from the Play Store. b. Register yourself via your Facebook or Google profile. c. Fill in your basic details to check your eligibility for availing a Personal Loan through proascent. This process hardly takes 3-5 minutes, after which you shall be shown an ‘eligibility successful’ page. An SMS indicating the same shall also be sent over to your registered mobile number (Sometimes, this process might take up to 24 hours in case there is a detailed check that is required. In such cases, you shall receive a call from proascent within 1 working day of application). d. Upon confirmation on the eligibility, you will need to upload your KYC documents and fill in a few more details about yourself. It should not take you more than 5-10 minutes to do the same. e. Once you have uploaded the KYC documents and the other additional information, the profile will be verified depending on the authenticity and quality of the documents uploaded. f. Once verified, you can apply for a loan through proascent and sign a Loan agreement. After the Loan agreement is signed and loan application is created, the amount shall be disbursed into the bank account provided by you within 15 minutes.
