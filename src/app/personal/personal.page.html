Personal Loan - Salaried

A Personal Loan option for Salaried folks,where you can avail amounts ranging from ₹10000 to ₹2 Lakh for a tenure ranging from 3 to 15 months. The bank transfer of the loan amount happens within 24 hours of loan application.All you need is your PAN Card, only one ID & Address Proof(either one of your Aadhaar/Voter ID/Passport),and your Salary Proof.

DOCUMENTS :

  PAN Card

  Address Proof(either of Aadhaar/Voter ID/ Passport/Driving License)

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA :

  Above 21 years of age

  Resident of India.

  Should have monthly source of income.

  Minimum family income of 15000.

LOAN AMOUNT :

  ₹10000 TO ₹200000

TENURE :

  From 3 to 15 Months
